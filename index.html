9. Meghalaya

Meghalaya is a hilly state in northeastern India. The name means "the abode of clouds" in Sanskrit. Famous for its high rainfall, subtropical forests and biodiversity, it is abutted by Assam in the north and east and by Bangladesh in the south. Popular Culture of Meghalaya is Rock Music, Cars and Western Fashion and Traditional Culture is something which is quite obscured from outsiders and it could be complicated to understand. Meghalaya belongs to three primary tribes: Khasi, Jaintia and Garos. All of them is Matricial, the society is Women oriented. The capital of Meghalaya is Shillong which is also a very beautiful place which you should must visit. One of the famous and local festival of Meghalaya is Wangala celebrated in the month of November every year Often referred to as the dance of a hundred drums, the festival marks the end of the period of labour in the hope of yielding a good harvest. Men, women and children from all age groups dress up for the festival and rejoice by dancing on the beats of long cylindrical drums. The art and craft of Meghalaya is also famous Apart from weaving Dakmanda- the fabric of their costumes, they are famous for making Tlieng, a special kind of cane mat. This mat is said to survive the test of time for as long as 20-30 years. Other than this, handwoven cane basket and chairs are unique to take from here. Go and explore Meghalaya and it's beautiful culture.
Best Time to visit : March to June.
